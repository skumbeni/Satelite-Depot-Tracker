# Executive Summary

SDMS Pro is an offline-first Progressive Web Application (PWA) developed to manage Food Reserve Agency Satellite Depots.

The system manages:

- Grain Purchases
- Grain Dispatch
- Inventory & Requisites
- Labour & Payroll
- Daily Depot Returns
- Reports
- WhatsApp Communication
- Audit Trail
- Backup & Restore

The application is designed to work completely offline and synchronize in future versions.

---

# Project Vision

To become Zambia's leading depot operations management platform by providing a simple, reliable and intelligent solution for managing satellite depots.

# Pilot Success Criteria

The pilot will be considered successful if:

- Daily Depot Return completed in under 10 minutes.
- Grain balances are always accurate.
- Inventory balances reconcile correctly.
- Payroll calculations are correct.
- Reports are accepted by the Marketing Assistant.
- WhatsApp summaries are successfully sent.
- No data loss occurs.
- Users are able to operate the application with minimal support.
